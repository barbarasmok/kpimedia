Are you overwhelmed trying to figure out how to maximize your online presence with SEO? Let us help you with a great first step—Google My Business

Google My Business, or GMB for short, is a free tool that lets you manage how your business appears on the Google map at the top of Google search pages. GMB is a vital way for customers to find you online, and it's an underused but critical marketing tool. We can help you claim, optimize and manage your Google My Business listing. The three most important reasons for GMB are:

  • controlling your business information
  • showing up in Google search results
  • managing reviews

The worst-case scenario is your GMB page is full of misinformation and bad reviews of your business. We can maximize your GMB listing and even serve as your constant GMB manager. And if you haven’t claimed your GMB page, we can help you set it up.

Reviews play a critical part in a business’s ability to differentiate itself from the competition and can impact rankings. And yes, your business should be monitoring and replying to reviews—both good ones and bad ones. Reviews matter! The more glowing reviews on your GMB listing, the more calls your business will get. And the more you respond to reviews, the more calls you will get. Here are five smart ways to manage reviews:

  • Respond to all reviews promptly as it encourages others to leave reviews
  • Respond to poor reviews and complaints honestly and diplomatically
  • Respect reviews as important content on your GMB page
  • Don’t obsess over 5-star reviews as customers want to see recent reviews
  • Ask past customers for reviews with specific suggestions and questions
