Frequently Asked Questions

I have a bricks-and-mortar store. Why do I need an online presence?

Most consumers begin their buying research online. Even if your company does not conduct business online, your customers and potential customers expect to see you online. An online presence builds your brand, increases your credibility and is cost-effective marketing. Most importantly, an online presence helps customers find you.

How do I get my business noticed by Google?

When someone searches for a service or product you provide, Google will list all the websites offering that service or product. The order of those websites can make or break your business. To get your business noticed by Google and thus higher up on the list, your content must be high quality and well written. Our writers know how to create the best BetterBook Landing Page for your business by using important keywords and headlines that help your Google ranking.

Is getting my website’s landing page on the first page of Google enough?

In a word—no. Why not? Getting a good search ranking on Google is a vital first step, but the work of getting quality leads and bookings for your business has just begun. Most small business websites fail to put much thought into what comes after the click. What turns readers into buyers? That’s where a high-converting landing page comes in. And that’s where a BetterBook Landing Page can help.

What’s the difference between a BetterBook one-page Landing Page and a multiple-page one?

For a one-page BetterBook Landing Page, all of your information and services are described and summarized on one page. For multiple-page Landing Pages, each major category of your services or products is described on a separate page. What are the advantages of this? Obviously, your products and services can be described in more detail on separate pages. But more importantly, during an online Google search, customers are much more likely to find exactly what they are searching for with the specificity of a separate landing page for each category of your products or services.
