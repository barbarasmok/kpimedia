Lead Generation Landing Page

What is a Landing Page?
Landing Pages are in-depth sales pages that answer questions that potential customers may have about your product or service. Landing Pages include a call-to-action form that will provide leads that lead to sales.
Do I need a Landing Page?
Ask yourself: Does your business have a sales page for every service you offer? If not, then your business is missing out on a great tool to turn potential customers into leads and sales. One way to determine if you have a Landing Page is to ask what link you provide when you purchase digital ads. If your “learn more” button goes to your home page or a general services page, then you could greatly increase the effectiveness of your ads by building a high-converting Landing Page.

Trust Story

What is a Trust Story?
A Trust Story is where one of our professional writers interviews the owner or manager in a friendly Q&A format that builds trust with the face behind the business. You could think of it as the ultimate “about us” story.
Do I need a Trust Story?
People like doing business with people they know and trust. That’s why one of the top read pages on your website is your “about us” page. Here is a chance to update that popular page with a professionally written story that will build trust in your local business.
Can you show me an example of a Trust Story?
  • Meet the new Cranbrook Farmers Market manager
Where will my Trust Story be seen?
Your Trust Story will run as a promoted story on one of our websites (choose from rvwest.com; snoriderswest.com; riderswestmag.com and kootenaybiz.com) and will be included in an eNewsletter and on all our corresponding social media channels.
You can also post the story on your “about us” page to enhance your website.

Reputation Story

What is a Reputation Story?
A Reputation Story is where one of our professional writers interviews one of your happy customers. It will use a story arc to share the customer’s buying journey, showing the problems and solutions, and how the customer feels about the end result. You could think of it as the ultimate customer review.
Do I need a Reputation Story?
Does your business have good reviews on Google or FaceBook? Have you ever wondered how to turn those reviews into a case study where your customers can vouch for your business? The best way to use five-star reviews is to turn them into effective Reputation Stories. If you provide the name of a satisfied customer, we can do the rest.
Can you show me an example of a Reputation Story?

  • Two Cranbrook homeowners share their stories and tips about buying their dream homes
Where will my Reputation Story be seen?
Your Reputation Story will run as a promoted story on one of our websites (choose from rvwest.com; snoriderswest.com; riderswestmag.com and kootenaybiz.com) and will be included in an eNewsletter and on all our corresponding social media channels.
You can also post the Reputation Story on your website as a blog post or testimonial.
